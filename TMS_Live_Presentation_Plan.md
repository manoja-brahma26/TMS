# Live Presentation Plan for Shortlisted Teams

## 1. Presentation Objective

The finalist presentation should show that the team understands the business problem, has a credible architecture, can execute in a brownfield environment, and can demonstrate meaningful value through concrete workflows.

## 2. Time Allocation

- **3 minutes:** proposal summary
- **7-8 minutes:** solution demos
- **4-5 minutes:** Q&A

## 3. Presentation Flow

### Part 1: Proposal Summary

Cover the following in the first 3 minutes:

- the current TMS challenge
- the future-state vision
- the 3Cs architecture approach
- the business value and transition strategy

### Part 2: Solution Demos

Use a connected story rather than separate disconnected screens.

Recommended flow:

1. employee creates trip request
2. system recommends itinerary and policy-compliant option
3. manager approval is routed and completed
4. agency booking coordination is shown
5. post-trip expense readiness is demonstrated

### Part 3: Q&A

Be prepared for questions on:

- why this is brownfield-ready
- how policy and approvals are controlled
- how AI decisions are grounded and governed
- what gets automated and what stays human-controlled
- timeline, cost, and rollout risk

## 4. Suggested Presenters

- **Presenter 1:** business value and proposal summary
- **Presenter 2:** architecture and workflow story during demos
- **Presenter 3:** technical or delivery expert for Q&A

## 5. Key Messages to Reinforce

- this approach improves travel without breaking current operations
- AI is governed, grounded, and phased
- the architecture is aligned to Persistent's 3Cs
- the solution creates a reusable platform beyond travel

## 6. Q&A Preparation Topics

- brownfield migration approach
- coexistence with email and agency workflows
- governance and responsible AI
- integration complexity and risk mitigation
- commercial realism and implementation phasing

## 7. Presentation Tips

- keep the summary business-first
- use demos to prove realism, not just UI polish
- call out policy checks and human control moments explicitly
- end by reinforcing the platform vision beyond travel
